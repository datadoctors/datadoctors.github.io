SaveCustomer Hook

The SaveCustomer hook is activated when a customer is saved to the database.

HookType value

11

Parameter value

5

The parameter passed to the hook will be the ID of the customer in the database.

The SaveCustomer hook is activated under the following conditions.

  • A new customer is added to the database.

  • An existing customer is changed.

Extending the Databases

Customizing RMS often requires you to extend the databases to store and retrieve custom information. Observe the following guidelines when extending the databases.

  • Do not add columns to existing RMS tables.

  • We recommend that you create a separate database to store your custom information.

  • Ensure that the names of your tables will not conflict with tables from other add-ins or future versions of RMS. We recommend that you prefix each of your table names with the name of your company to create a unique name.

Numeric Expression

A numeric expression is a literal number, a variable that evaluates to a number, or a mathematical expression. The following are examples of numeric expressions.

  • 1.0

  • PageWidth

  • PageWidth - 5.0

The supported mathematical operators, in order of precedence, are the following. Operators in the same table row have the same precedence. Note that the "<" and ">" characters are reserved in XML; the escape sequences for these characters is shown in the table.

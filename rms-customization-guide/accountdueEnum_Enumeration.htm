accountdueEnum Enumeration

The accountdueEnum enumerated type defines the methods used to calculate the date a customer account is due.

Public Enum accountdueEnum

  accountdueCloseOfBillingCyclePlusGracePeriod = 1

  accountdueAccountReceivableDatePlusGracePeriod = 2

End Enum

Constants

accountdueCloseOfBillingCyclePlusGracePeriod

The due date for the customer account is the day of the month specified as the end of the billing cycle plus the grace period.

accountdueAccountReceivableDatePlusGracePeriod

The due date for the customer account is the date that the account receivable is due plus the grace period.

Remarks

The accountdueEnum enumeration is used by the Customer.AccountDateDueType property.

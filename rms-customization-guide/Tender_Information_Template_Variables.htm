Tender Information Template Variables

The following receipt template variables contain information about the tenders used in the transaction. Variables that reference a collection must be passed a key to the desired entry.

 

Variable

Description

Tender.AmountIn

Total amount of the specified tender taken in during the transaction.

Tender.AmountOut

Total amount of the specified tender paid out as change during the transaction.

Tender.AmountInlocal

Total amount of the specified tender taken in during the transaction, in the local currency.

Tender.AmountOutlocal

Total amount of the specified tender paid out as change during the transaction, in the local currency.

Tender.AmountInRounding

Amount of the local currency left over when the amount of the specified tender taken in during the transaction is converted to its equivalent in local currency. This variable is also used for currencies with RoundTo values.

Tender.AmountOutRounding

Amount of the local currency left over when the amount of the specified tender paid out as change during the transaction is converted to its equivalent in local currency. This variable is also used for currencies with RoundTo values.

Tender.AccountNumber

Check, credit card, or debit card number.

Tender.AccountNumberMasked

Check, credit card, or debit card number with all but the last four digits replaced with the "X" character.

Tender.ApprovalCode

Approval code for credit and debit cards.

Tender.Expiration

Expiration date for credit cards.

Tender.StateCode

State code for the check.

Tender.Swiped

Boolean value that indicates whether the credit or debit card was entered by passing the card through the card reader.

Tender.Track1

Information contained on the first track of the credit or debit card.

Tender.Track2

Information contained on the first track of the credit or debit card.

Tender.VoucherNumber

Number of the voucher.

Tender.VoucherPreviousBalance

Balance on the voucher before the current transaction.

Tender.VoucherNewBalance

Balance on the voucher, including the current transaction.

Tender.VoucherPreviousBalanceLocal

Balance on the voucher before the current transaction, in the local currency.

Tender.VoucherNewBalanceLocal

Balance on the voucher, including the current transaction, in the local currency.

Tender.Descriptor.Description

Description of the tender.

Tender.Descriptor.ExchangeRate

Exchange rate for the tender.

Tender.Descriptor.TenderType

Type of the tender. This is one of the following values:

  • TenderCreditCard

  • TenderAccount

  • TenderCash

  • TenderCheck

  • TenderECCashCard

  • TenderFoodStamp

  • TenderVoucher

  • TenderOther

Tender.Descriptor.RequireSig

Boolean value that indicates whether the tender requires a signature.

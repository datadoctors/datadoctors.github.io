About Reports

Microsoft® Retail Management System provides extensive reporting capabilities through its own specialized reports, called Active Reports, as well as through Crystal Reports. Store Operations and Headquarters ship with a wide variety of reports. These reports can be modified by the manager to implement additional functionality, such as showing or hiding columns or changing the grouping. In addition, completely new reports can be generated. This feature is especially useful for extending the functionality of customized add-ins.

We recommend Active Reports as a first choice. However, consider using Crystal Reports when a form layout is preferred over a tabular layout. Active Reports are limited to a tabular layout.

This section describes how to create new Active Reports and set up templates for Crystal Reports. For more information, see Active Reports and Crystal Reports.

To see how the loyalty add-in uses reports, see Loyalty Report Sample.

CIRCLE Element

The CIRCLE element displays a circle on the receipt.

Attributes

None

Value

None

Child Elements

Element

Content

Description

X1

Numeric expression

Required. X coordinate of the center of the circle, in inches from the left edge of the receipt.

Y1

Numeric expression

Required. Y coordinate of the center of the circle, in inches from the top edge of the receipt.

RADIUS

Numeric expression

Required. Radius of the circle in inches.

PENCOLOR

Color

Optional. Color of the border of the circle.

PENSTYLE

  • psSolid

  • psDash

  • psDot

  • psDashDot

  • psDashDotDot

  • psTransparent

  • psInsideSolid

Optional. Pen style of the border of the circle. This element is not used if the value of the PENWIDTH element is greater than one.

PENWIDTH

Numeric expression

Optional. Width of the border of the circle.

BRUSHCOLOR

Color

Optional. Fill color of the circle.

Remarks

Shapes are drawn in the order in which they appear in the receipt template. Shapes that overlap existing shapes appear on top.

X and Y coordinates are absolute locations on the receipt, and are not affected by other elements on the receipt template.

Example

The following example displays a green circle with a blue dash-dot border on the receipt. The circle has a diameter of one inch, and is displayed in the center of the receipt with its top edge 0.5 inches from the top of the receipt.

<SET name="PageWidth" custom="true" description="Width of the page" type="vbString">

    2.75

</SET>

 

<CIRCLE>

    <RADIUS>     0.5           </RADIUS>

    <X1>         PageWidth / 2 </X1>

    <Y1>         1             </Y1>      

    <PENCOLOR>   vbBlue        </PENCOLOR>

    <PENSTYLE>   psDashDot     </PENSTYLE>

    <PENWIDTH>   1             </PENWIDTH>

    <BRUSHCOLOR> vbGreen       </BRUSHCOLOR>

</CIRCLE>

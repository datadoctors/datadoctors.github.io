pricesourceEnum Enumeration

The pricesourceEnum enumerated type defines the possible sources of the price.

Public Enum pricesourceEnum

  pricesourceRetail = 1

  pricesourceQuantityDiscount = 2

  pricesourceBuydown = 3

  pricesourceLevel = 4

  pricesourceSale = 5

  pricesourceCashierDiscountFromRetail = 6

  pricesourceCashierDiscountFromCurrentPrice = 7

  pricesourceCashierDiscountMarkupFromCost = 8

  pricesourceCashierDiscountProfitMargin = 9

  pricesourceCashierSet = 10   

  pricesourceComponent = 11  

  pricesourceLevelA = 12

  pricesourceLevelB = 13

  pricesourceLevelC = 14  

  pricesourceDiscountFromRetail = 15

  pricesourceDiscountFromCurrentPrice = 16

  pricesourceDiscountMarkupFromCost = 17

  pricesourceDiscountProfitMargin = 18

End Enum

Constants

pricesourceRetail

The price was calculated automatically as the retail price.

pricesourceQuantityDiscount

The price was calculated automatically as the Quantity Discount price.

pricesourceBuydown

The price was calculated automatically as the Buydown price.

pricesourceLevel

Not used; this value has been replaced by the pricesourceLevelA, pricesourceLevelB, and pricesourceLevelC values.

pricesourceSale

The price was calculated automatically as the sale price.

pricesourceCashierDiscountFromRetail

The cashier applied a percentage discount to the retail price.

pricesourceCashierDiscountFromCurrentPrice

The cashier applied a percentage discount to the current price.

pricesourceCashierDiscountMarkupFromCost

The cashier calculated the price as a percentage markup from the cost.

pricesourceCashierDiscountProfitMargin

The cashier adjusted the price to produce a specified profit margin.

pricesourceCashierSet  

The price was set by the cashier.

pricesourceComponent

The price of the kit item was calculated as the sum of the prices for the component items in the kit.

pricesourceLevelA

The price is the Level A price.

pricesourceLevelB

The price is the Level B price.

pricesourceLevelC

The price is the Level C price.

pricesourceDiscountFromRetail

A percentage of the retail price was discounted from the item.

pricesourceDiscountFromCurrentPrice

A percentage of the current price was discounted from the item.

pricesourceDiscountMarkupFromCost

The price was calculated as a percentage markup from the cost.

pricesourceDiscountProfitMargin

The price was adjusted to produce a specified profit margin.

Remarks

The pricesourceEnum enumeration is used by the TransactionEntry.PriceSource property.

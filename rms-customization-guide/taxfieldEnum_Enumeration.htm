taxfieldEnum Enumeration

The taxfieldEnum enumerated type defines the fields used to calculate tax on a per-customer basis.

Public Enum taxfieldEnum

  taxfieldState = 1

  taxfieldcountry = 2

  taxfieldZipCode = 3

  taxfieldCity = 4

  taxfieldShipToState = 5

  taxfieldShipToCountry = 6

  taxfieldShipToZipCode = 7

  taxfieldShipToCity = 8

  taxfieldCustom1 = 9

  taxfieldCustom2 = 10

  taxfieldCustom3 = 11

  taxfieldCustom4 = 12

  taxfieldCustom5 = 13

End Enum

Constants

taxfieldState

Tax for the customer is based on the state in which they reside.

taxfieldcountry

Tax for the customer is based on the country in which they reside.

taxfieldZipCode

Tax for the customer is based on their ZIP code.

taxfieldCity

Tax for the customer is based on the city in which they reside.

taxfieldShipToState

Tax for the customer is based on the state in their shipping address.

taxfieldShipToCountry

Tax for the customer is based on the country in their shipping address.

taxfieldShipToZipCode

Tax for the customer is based on the ZIP code in their shipping address.

taxfieldShipToCity

Tax for the customer is based on the city in their shipping address.

taxfieldCustom1

Tax for the customer is based on the first user-defined field.

taxfieldCustom2

Tax for the customer is based on the second user-defined field.

taxfieldCustom3

Tax for the customer is based on the third user-defined field.

taxfieldCustom4

Tax for the customer is based on the fourth user-defined field.

taxfieldCustom5

Tax for the customer is based on the fifth user-defined field.

Remarks

The custom tax fields are the custom text fields defined in the Custom Captions dialog box accessible through the Manager configuration.

The taxfieldEnum enumeration is used by the Configuration.TaxField property.

Loyalty Add-in Status Display

The loyalty program status display shows loyalty information once a customer is loaded. The information displayed depends on the loyalty status of the loaded customer as follows:

  • Customer not enrolled: Displays the POS status bar prompt defined in Manager; the default message is "Invite the customer to join the loyalty program."

  • Customer enrolled: Displays the following loyalty information:

  • Date on which the customer joined the loyalty program

  • Customer's current loyalty level

  • Customer's next loyalty level

  • Purchase amount required for the customer to reach the next loyalty level

  • Customer's current loyalty discount
